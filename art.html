My Art

The reason I got into dance is because of a show called dance moms. It's a show where seven little girls dancing at the studio together and they go to competitions every weekend. I was very inspired by them so I started to teach myself how to dance, and I joined a studio of my own. this is important to me because it lets me express myself without using words, it's freeing to dance, especially when I'm going through something.
